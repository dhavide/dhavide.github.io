My research lies in the area of scientific computing (what traditionally would be called numerical analysis). I am specifically interested in approximation techniques and algorithms related to the solution of large-scale computational inverse problems and numerical continuation problems. Applications I have worked on include includes medical imaging (e.g., reconstruction algorithms in x-ray computed tomography and magnetic resonance imaging), forensic bloodstain pattern analysis, and geophysical prospecting. Other broader areas of interest include high-performance (i.e., parallel) computing methodologies, numerical solution of partial differential equations, iterative methods for the solution of linear and nonlinear systems of equations, and hybrid symbolic-numeric algorithms.
